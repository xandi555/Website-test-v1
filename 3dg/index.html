Project intro

I have to say that this was one of my favorite Exercises in my Bachelor studies, not only because it was the first deepdive I had in the artistic approach, but also because it focused so much on the human machine to create, to see, too understand.
In the first Chapers we used self build Tools to develop a font. The circumstances should determine the style writing.

A sweater for a furry friend

After the Analog work, I went into the digital, do compile the Font seen on the right. After that i went back and made i Lino cut out of it.

Phase III.

The developt sont now was abstracted further on. The facinascion in moving to understand a object, brought me to this idea.
I rebuilt the idea in 3D in my PC to see if the Idea could work out. On my quest for the material translation into matter I stumbled upon the Great Master Alberto Biasi, who did it so much better. But still I wanted to understand his Process. So I did.

Phase IV.

Phase IV brings us back to the idea of the third phase, in which the viewer
is forced to move in order to understand the object in its entirety. Only this time, the object almost dissolves and becomes unrecognizable as soon as it is viewed by the moving observer. Just like real time. The more you really want to understand it, the more incomprehensible it seems.
