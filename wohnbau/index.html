Project intro

This project focuses on affordable and social housing. This should not only provide individual well-being, but also promote collective well-being and create space for the future. A place that is geared towards change and dynamism and is not overwhelmed by ever-new challenges.

A sweater for a furry friend

Smaller core Area. For a bright Epidermis

The building is pretty simple with the Boxy approach in the Lower Levels.
This allows maximum use to be made of the
built-up volume. On the upper floors, however, this drive for efficiency eases off. Generous terraces invite residents to linger and chat with their neighbors. As this area can be opened in summer and closed in winter, this is possible all year round. Further up, the space becomes increasingly communal, and on the roof, three apartment's takes care of the communal garden or lounge area, depending on what is needed at the time. 

Adapteble Frontcourt



The structure of this building can be freely adapted to any possible dimensions of another plot of land or requirements. Axis spacing, path width, floor height, etc. are flexible.

Another feature is the detachable panels, which can be used to create communal or more private spaces depending on the needs of the residents or the season.
