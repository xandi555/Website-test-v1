Project Intro

The Analysis of the Tagore Memorial Center has sparked interest in the construction of carefully designt engineer-concrete-structures. Reducing material usage through Geometry was the scope. Side effect like in Nature is always beautyful to look at, because it works.

Innovative Shading

Lightweight ETFE air cushion

The entire surfaces between the Freeform Concrete Slabs are closed with the strong & durable ETFE-Sheets. In addition they are filled with air to guarantee Insulation in the cold months & shading in the hotter ones.

Section through the Model

The Structure is an omage to big barrel vault’s and to furtheron safe on wights the prefabricated construction is connected and short-circuited in the foundation area. In This way all the Forces are closed in a circle.


After entering from the south side, there is a reception area from which you can reach the side exhibition areas or climb a spiral staircase to the first floor.
This is clamped between two arches and rests on the core of the spiral staircase, as well as at the entrance to the first floor of the theater cube. The beams, which merge into the floor slab towards the center, were created in a computer-aided form optimization study and are intended to reflect the theme of efficiency and integration.
The floor slab is supported by two arches, which are connected to the staircase and the entrance area. The beams are supported by the arches and the staircase, and the floor slab is supported by the arches and the staircase.
