The Building

How can a massive Plot of Land in the city, where currently only cars are „living" be revitized and brought back to the people, animals and plants ?

First: use the old Riverbed as Tunnel for the Cars and bring up the River to the walking level.
Second: Grow a lot of Bamboo
Third: Use the Bamboo to build a sustainable Structure to grow Food in the building.

Through the help of the City the Project aims to educate and dealienate the Population for all coming generatiopns. 

Structure optimaziation

Grasshopper - Karamba 3D

To respond to such innovative approaches at the urban planning level, the Structure of this floating snake meandering through the 5.,6.,15. &12. District of Vienna had to be fit for the future too.
The different uses inside the building gave the the width and height of the building and the optimization came op with the most effiecient packing of the Bamboo-trusses.


Creating a 2nd Cityfloor

Since the building is elevated, there is a lot of protected open public Space, places to go for a swim in the River, meadows where children can play, trees where Birds chirp & a thriving Market for the local population. 
Some of the Areas in the Building are strictly reservated for education purposes where schools can come and learn about the cultivation of vegetables.
