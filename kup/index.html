Project intro

This is a smaller project, but no less interesting. As part of my work for the Kunst 2 Institute, where I mainly worked in the model-making workshop, in this case I had to design a reusable “display” that could be dismantled and reused in other contexts. For this semsester they should carry wearables wich the students had designed. With the pipe connections and the 3D-printed plugs specially designed for these aluminum pipes, this was possible without screws or drill holes.

A multifunctional Scipt

Designed for the ultimative versatility

The dowel consists of two different materials:
TPU&PLA. The first is a rubber-like plastic that is easier to print with a 3D printer. It was used to be compressed and ensure a stable connection. The hard plastic components were used to attach acrylic glass rods.
These two components are secured against twisting by the nubs on the top.
