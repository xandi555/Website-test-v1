Project intro

Future comes, and doesn’t wait to bring us ever new challanges. One of wich will be our Foodsupply.
Steadly the nutrients in our vegetables go down, while production cost, use of fertilizer, non locality and pesticides go up ! All in all alienation us from the one thing we really need to survive.

How can we bring back the connection to our Food ?

Highly adaptable Structure

One Design. Seven Buildings.
Thousand functions.

Through the formfinding Process it came to be that a highly modular and adaptive „Grid“ was needed. I achived this with a simpple line struchture in Rhino, and build from that Grid my Grasshopper Script. During the Design-Process I always could play around with the Volumes without ever redrawing any plans.
